About Founder

Nikita Beniwal
Founder and Industry Veteran

Nikita Beniwal, the visionary founder of Elements of India, is a seasoned professional with over 20 years of experience in the textile industry. With a strong foundation in textile chemistry engineering, her illustrious career spans diverse roles, each contributing to her deep expertise and leadership.

Her journey began as an R&D executive in a composite textile mill, where she honed her technical acumen. Over the years, she expanded her portfolio to include quality assurance, merchandising for manufacturing and export sectors, and heading product categories for prominent sourcing offices. In 2018, driven by her passion for personalized and customer-centric services, she established Elements of India. The company has since become a premier sourcing platform, delivering tailored solutions and exceptional value to its clients.

Under her guidance, a dedicated team of 16 professionals works passionately to achieve excellence in every project the company undertakes.

Beyond her professional achievements, Nikita is deeply committed to social causes. She actively collaborates with NGOs on initiatives promoting women empowerment, environmental sustainability, and the conservation of natural resources. Her steadfast dedication ensures that Elements of India not only excels in business but also contributes meaningfully to society.
