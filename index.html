What documentation is required for import-export transactions?

For import-export transactions, the following documentation is typically required:

  • Commercial Invoice: A detailed invoice outlining the goods being traded.
  • Packing List: A comprehensive list of items included in the shipment.
  • Bill of Lading: A legal document between the shipper and carrier detailing the type and quantity of goods.
  • Certificate of Origin: A document certifying the country of origin of the goods.
  • Import/Export License: Required licenses depending on the type of goods being traded.
Ensuring that you have all necessary documentation in order helps facilitate smooth customs clearance and compliance with local regulations.

How can I track my shipment?

Tracking your shipment is easy! Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to monitor the status of your shipment in real time. This ensures transparency and keeps you updated on the delivery progress, helping you plan accordingly.
